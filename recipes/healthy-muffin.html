Healthy Muffin

Sweet and Savery oatmeal muffin

If you are a college or university student and have been looking for an easy but delicious recipe for a sweet snack then you have come to the right place. As a student time is an importent commodety and we would not want to spend 1 hour 30 minutes in the kitchen preping and baking. So get your note book out or whatever trusted note taker you use and jot down this easy recipe.

NOTE:This recipe is in no way restrictive, so feel free to add fruits or different types of toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cup of flower
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 500 ml of milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanila essence (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon of oil (any oil can work)
  • 50 g of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of oats (you can opt for any type of topping like seeds or berries)

Steps

  1. Pour the flour, into a bowl and add your salt, baking powder, sugar into the bowl
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in well
  3. Then in a separate bowl microwave your butter until melted
  4. Add in your eggs and wisk well
  5. Proceed to add your milk, vanila essence and oil
  6. Mix in the wet ingredients well
  7. Pour in your wet ingredients mixture into the dry indgredients
  8. Then use a spoon to mix in the ingredients well, note that you can pour your wet mixture in batches instead of pouring everything in
  9. Pre-heat your oven to 100 degrees and wait for 10 minutes, this accelerates the baking process
  10. Then place your mixture into a baking tray and sprinkle the oats over the dough, then place the tray in the oven at 150 degrees and let it bake for 20 minutes
  11. Once the muffin has baked through, meaning that if you insert a tooth pick into the muffin it comes out clean, leave to rest for 5 minutes
