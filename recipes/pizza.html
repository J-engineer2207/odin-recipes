Delicious Pizza

Pizza is a beloved comfort food that's both delicious and customizable, making it the perfect meal for any occasion. Whether you're in the mood for a classic Margherita, a veggie-packed delight, or a meat lover's feast, pizza allows you to get creative with toppings while still keeping things simple. It's easy to prepare, even in a college kitchen, and you can make it with store-bought dough or from scratch if you're up for it. Plus, pizza can be as quick as a 15-minute bake or a slow-cooked masterpiece, depending on your time and effort.

Making pizza at home also gives you control over the ingredients, allowing you to choose fresher, healthier options compared to takeout. You can top your pizza with a variety of vegetables, cheeses, and proteins to suit your taste, and even make personal-sized pizzas for yourself or a group. With minimal ingredients—dough, sauce, cheese, and toppings—you can create a pizza that's both satisfying and easy to customize to your cravings, making it the ultimate college-friendly meal that's perfect for dinner or sharing with friends.

Ingrediants

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (or marinara sauce)
  • 1 to 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1-2 teaspoons olive oil (for brushing the crust)
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.) Dried oregano or basil (optional, for seasoning)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it heats up.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. Transfer it to a parchment paper-lined baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce. Add Parmesan cheese if desired.
  5. Arrange your chosen toppings evenly over the cheese.
  6. Sprinkle dried oregano or basil over the top for extra flavor.
  7. Lightly brush the exposed crust with olive oil to ensure a golden and crispy finish.
  8. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  9. Once baked, remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a minute, then slice and serve hot.
